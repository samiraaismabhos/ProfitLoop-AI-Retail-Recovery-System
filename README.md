# 🚀 ProfitLoop AI  
### Autonomous Closed-Loop Retail Recovery System

> Transforming operational retail waste into intelligent profit recovery.

---

## 📌 What is ProfitLoop AI?

ProfitLoop AI is an AI-powered operational recovery system designed for multi-branch retail chains.

The system continuously monitors branch-level inventory risks in real time and autonomously selects the most profitable recovery strategy before products become waste.

Unlike traditional retail analytics tools, ProfitLoop AI does not stop at reporting.

It closes the loop.

---

# 🔄 Closed-Loop Recovery Architecture

Detection → Optimization → Decision → Execution → Recovery

Every operational action feeds back into the system for continuous optimization.

---

# ⚡ Core Capabilities

## 🍓 Expiry Risk Detection
Detects products at risk of becoming waste using:
- expiry urgency
- stock imbalance
- sales velocity

---

## 💸 Dynamic Markdown Optimization
AI selects the most profitable discount level automatically.

Example:
- 10%
- 25%
- 40%

The system chooses the highest recovery outcome.

---

## 🚛 Smart Redistribution
When another branch has stronger demand, ProfitLoop AI automatically recommends redistribution.

The system evaluates:
- branch demand
- inventory pressure
- logistics feasibility
- recovery profitability

Transfers are executed only when:

Recovery Profit > Logistics Cost

---

## 🛣 Route-Aware Logistics Optimization
ProfitLoop AI integrates redistribution into Bravo’s existing delivery routes and unused truck capacity.

This creates:
- near-zero additional logistics cost
- near-zero extra fuel consumption
- lower CO₂ emissions

---

## 🎯 Loyalty Recovery Engine
AI-powered campaigns are launched automatically through:
- Bravo App
- BirBonus
- Umico

Discounts are personalized using:
- purchase history
- shopping behavior
- category preferences
- cashback activity
